Overview

Purpose

The Caloocan Chamber of Commerce serves as a catalyst for economic growth, a leader in community engagement, and a champion for local businesses. Our mission is to support and promote businesses through tailored member services, fostering economic and community development, and advocating for public policies that ensure sustainable prosperity for the Caloocan City region.

Audience

Whether you're a small business owner, entrepreneur, or a corporate leader, the Caloocan Chamber of Commerce is here to support your business success. We provide valuable resources, networking opportunities, and advocacy for businesses of all sizes. No matter your industry or experience, our goal is to empower you with the tools and connections you need to thrive in Caloocan Cityâ€™s dynamic economic landscape.
