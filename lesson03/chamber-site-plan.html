Purpose

The Caloocan Chamber of Commerce serves as a catalyst for economic growth, a leader in community engagement, and a champion for local businesses. Our mission is to support and promote businesses through tailored member services, fostering economic and community development, and advocating for public policies that ensure sustainable prosperity for the Caloocan City region.
